# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

expect-us is a small, focused collective. We hold ourselves to a simple standard: act with integrity and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and honest.
- Respect the work and time of others.
- Disagree constructively — attack ideas, not people.
- Keep discussions on-topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.
- Deliberately malicious or deceptive contributions.
- Any behavior that undermines the integrity of the project.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in removal from the project without warning.
Report issues to the project maintainer: [@hz8n](https://github.com/hz8n)

*We do not forgive. We do not forget.*
